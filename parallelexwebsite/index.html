MONKEY D. LUFFY

Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and the main protagonist of One Piece. His lifelong dream is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure left behind by the late Pirate King, Gol D. Roger. After accidentally eating the Gomu Gomu no Mi (Gum-Gum Fruit), a Devil Fruit that turned his body into rubber, Luffy gained the ability to stretch his body like rubber.

Devil Fruit

Gomu Gomu no Mi (Gum-Gum Fruit)

Bounty

3,000,000,000 Berries

Position

Captain

Known for his indomitable will, boundless optimism, and unwavering loyalty to his friends, Luffy consistently challenges the World Government and the strongest pirates in the world. His signature attack "Gomu Gomu no Pistol" and his straw hat, which was given to him by Shanks, have become iconic symbols in the world of One Piece.

RORONOA ZORO

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro is the combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of Luffy's top fighters. A formidable swordsman, Zoro aims to become the greatest swordsman in the world, a promise he made to his childhood friend Kuina. His signature three-sword style, where he holds one sword in each hand and one in his mouth, makes him a unique and deadly opponent.

Fighting Style

Santoryu (Three Sword Style)

Bounty

1,111,000,000 Berries

Position

Swordsman/First Mate

Despite his tough exterior, Zoro is fiercely loyal to Luffy and has shown willingness to sacrifice himself for his captain and crew multiple times. His terrible sense of direction has become a running joke in the series, often getting lost even when following others. Zoro's determination and strict training regimen are a testament to his dedication to his dream.

VINSMOKE SANJI

Vinsmoke Sanji

Vinsmoke Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the crew's top fighters. Born into the infamous Vinsmoke Family, a powerful family of assassins, Sanji rejected his heritage to pursue his dream of finding the All Blue, a legendary sea where all four seas (North, South, East, West) meet, containing every fish from each of the four seas.

Fighting Style

Black Leg Style

Bounty

1,032,000,000 Berries

Position

Cook

Sanji is a chivalrous man who refuses to harm women under any circumstances, a principle instilled in him by his mentor, "Red Leg" Zeff. As the ship's cook, he ensures that the crew eats nutritious meals and never wastes food, due to his childhood experience with starvation. Sanji's signature fighting style involves powerful kicks, allowing him to keep his hands safe for cooking.

NAMI

Nami

Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the smartest members of the crew. Her dream is to draw a map of the entire world. Initially joining the crew to pay off her debt to Arlong, she later became a loyal member after Luffy helped free her village.

Weapon

Clima-Tact

Bounty

366,000,000 Berries

Position

Navigator

Known for her exceptional navigation skills and love for money, Nami is the crew's financial manager. Her dream of mapping the world drives her to explore every island they visit. Despite her initial reluctance to fight, she has grown into a capable fighter using the Clima-Tact, a weapon that manipulates weather.

USOPP

Usopp

Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates and a skilled inventor. His dream is to become a brave warrior of the sea. Despite his initial cowardice, he has grown into a reliable crew member who always comes through when his friends are in need.

Weapon

Kabuto

Bounty

500,000,000 Berries

Position

Sniper

Usopp's incredible marksmanship and inventive mind make him a valuable asset to the crew. His tendency to exaggerate stories and his initial cowardice are balanced by his unwavering loyalty and his ability to overcome his fears when his friends are in danger.

NICO ROBIN

Nico Robin

Nico Robin is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the few people who can read the Poneglyphs. Her dream is to find the True History of the Void Century. After being hunted by the World Government for most of her life, she found a new home with the Straw Hat Pirates.

Devil Fruit

Hana Hana no Mi

Bounty

930,000,000 Berries

Position

Archaeologist

Robin's calm and collected personality, combined with her vast knowledge of history and ancient languages, makes her an invaluable member of the crew. Her Devil Fruit ability allows her to sprout parts of her body anywhere, making her both a formidable fighter and a useful utility character.
